What is Self-Care?

Self-care involves activities that promote mental, physical, and emotional health.

Benefits

Improved mood, reduced stress, and better health are key benefits of self-care.

Activities

Examples include exercise, meditation, hobbies, and setting boundaries.

Consistency

Regular self-care helps build resilience and better mental health over time.
