What is Depression?

Depression is more than sadness. It is a persistent feeling of hopelessness that affects daily life.

Causes

Biological, psychological, and environmental factors contribute to depression.

Symptoms

Symptoms include loss of interest, fatigue, and changes in sleep or appetite.

Treatment

Counseling, medication, and support groups are effective ways to treat depression.
