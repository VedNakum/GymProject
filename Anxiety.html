Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety affects millions worldwide. Learn its causes, effects, and how to manage it effectively.

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a normal response to stress but can become overwhelming and disruptive. It's characterized by persistent feelings of worry or fear about everyday situations.

Causes

Genetics, brain chemistry, trauma, and environmental factors can all contribute to anxiety disorders.

Symptoms

Common symptoms include restlessness, rapid breathing, racing thoughts, and difficulty concentrating.

Management

Techniques such as mindfulness, therapy, regular exercise, and medication can help manage anxiety effectively.
