What is Stress?

Stress is the body's response to pressure. While normal, excessive stress can affect health.

Triggers

Work, relationships, finances, and health issues are common stress triggers.

Effects

Chronic stress can lead to physical and emotional exhaustion, anxiety, and depression.

Relief

Practice relaxation techniques, exercise, and time management to reduce stress.
